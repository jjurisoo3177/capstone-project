What is ExpandingCraft?

Welcome, wanderers! ExpandingCraft is a friendly survival Minecraft server.
Originally we started out with a small world border of 4x4 chunks. This has been steadily expanding over the last year. We have seen many players, done number of community projects and created the lore for our server. We hope you have a wonderful stay, if you decide to play with us!

Playing on Expandingcraft

We support most playing styles! Most of our players like to hop on here and there, and enjoy making their own builds. We also have some community areas and resources near the spawn. However disruptive gameplay is not supported.
Over time the server has accumulated quite a number of builds, some made by cooperation, many by individual players. If you like a relaxing building experience, you are more than welcome!

Technical info:

The server runs fabric with lithium and starlight. We also use Proximity Chat, Dynmap and No chat reports.
Notable datapacks: dragon elytra drop, fast leaf decay, one player sleep.
Currently we allow all client mods except baritone and similar.
